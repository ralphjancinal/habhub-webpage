Our Story

The history of Hab Hub family resto bar begins when Torks and Xiao met at a local resto bar and they notice that the place lacks improvement and facilities to make the customers enjoy their stay.

After a long discussion they decided to renovate and make the place more lively and named it "Hab Hub" that means a place to eat happily without worries. Afterwhile it became a famous resto in our town.
